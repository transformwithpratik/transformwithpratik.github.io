My Story

From struggle to coach.

8+ YEARS COACHING

I'm Pratik — and I get it.

I started lifting in college weighing 95kg with zero clue what I was doing. Crash diets, broscience, "magic" supplements — I tried it all and burned out fast.

What changed everything was learning the science: progressive overload, calorie tracking, recovery, and most importantly — building a system I could stick to forever.

Today, I help busy professionals, students, and parents do exactly that: lose fat, build strength, and reclaim their energy without giving up the foods or life they love.
